How to play

The game consists of three levels, easy, medium and challenging. Each level consists of cards that should be flipped to reveal the images hidden underneath. The aim is to find and click on all of pairs of similar images before the time runs out. The more challenging levels have more cards and a little bit of extra time to find the pairs. Good luck and have fun!
